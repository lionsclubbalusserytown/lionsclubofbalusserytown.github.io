About Us

The Lions Club of Balussery Town is part of the world’s largest service organization — Lions Clubs International. Since our founding in 2017, we have been committed to humanitarian service and supporting the local community.

Our Mission

To empower volunteers to serve their communities, meet humanitarian needs, encourage peace, and promote understanding through Lions clubs.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in community and humanitarian service.
